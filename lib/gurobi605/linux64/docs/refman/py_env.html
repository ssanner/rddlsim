Env

Gurobi environment object. Note that environments play a much smaller role in the Python interface than they do in other Gurobi language APIs. You will rarely need to work with environments. The two primary exceptions are when you use concurrent environments in one of the concurrent optimizers, or when you manage multiple Gurobi Compute Servers.
